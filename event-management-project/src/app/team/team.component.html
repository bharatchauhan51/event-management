About our Founder and Team
Mr. Varun Jain is not only the event’s founder. He is also an ISBF alumnus with a degree in Business Management.
Currently working as a risk analyst at ‘EY’, his motivation is to bring public speaking to various backgrounds and eliminate the exclusion attached to it
Having done many speaking events himself, Varun correctly comprehends the nervousness and fear that arises during public speaking. By heading an event as novel as IPSS, he hopes that people both afraid and unafraid of public speaking feel comfortable to bring forth their delightful sets of opinions onto the forum and learn memorable lessons from the event.
